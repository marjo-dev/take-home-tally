The Settings page is where you configure your app preferences and manage your data. There are three cards in this section: Deductions, Role Manager, and Backup / Restore.

The Deductions card is where you can set your tax rates, retirement contributions, employer match, and any other deductions.

The Role Manager card is where you can add or edit your roles and hourly rates. These roles will appear in the Daily Entry dropdown menu when you log your shift.

The Backup / Restore card is where you can backup your data, export your data to a spreadsheet, or restore from a backup.

üí∞ Deductions

The values in this card are saved locally and used to estimate your weekly take-home pay.

By default, this app applies a 20% tax deduction to taxable income, and I recommend keeping it here for now. I'm working on a future enhancement to automatically calculate your tax rate using federal and state tax brackets, which should provide a more accurate estimate.

If you don't don't contribute to a 401(k) or Roth 401(k), you can leave those at 0% and the employer match won't apply either. The same goes for other optional deductions.

Note: This app provides estimates only ‚Äî it doesn't calculate your exact taxes or retirement contributions. Your actual paycheck may differ based on your employer's payroll system and your own tax withholdings.
